About

Plutus industries LTD.

Plutus industries Ltd. was established in March, 2021. Mr. Bharat Shivnani and Mrs. Rita Shivnani created the company together to establish their shared goal to build a business that genuinely symbolizes their own beliefs. The meaning of PISTIS according to Roman Fides means A goddess personifying trust, faith and trustworthiness. This trust serves as a foundation for this business and for the company's values, that being - faith in their practices, workers and commodities. Pistis Global DMCC strives to use its market expertise in all facets of its operations and it hopes to apply their wealth of knowledge to provide the polymers and chemical sectors with high-quality products and services

The business is involved in the import and export of several chemical and polymers. We pride ourselves on having excellent relationships with our suppliers, which allow us to acquire plastic raw materials and chemicals from all over the world at the most competitive prices.
